Basotho Music & Dance

Music and dance are the heartbeat of the Basotho people. They tell stories, preserve history, and unite communities during weddings, harvests, and ceremonies.

Experience the Rhythm

Press play to enjoy the sounds and movements that have carried our culture for generations.

Graceful women's dance performed on knees.

Mohobelo Dance

Lesiba

Thomo

Women’s string instrument for storytelling.

Lekope (Rattles)

Shaken to add texture to performances.

Seholoholo (Whistle)

Signals rhythm changes and energizes crowds.
